How It Works

Create Fundraiser

Create your fundraiser in a few simple steps. Share your story and passion. Then upload some pictures to give a face to your cause!

Share Your Cause

After you have created your fundraiser, you can share with your family, friends, and followers. Encourage them to share and you get the idea, before you know it, the world will know your passion.

Collect Donations

Once your family, friends, and followers know your story, they have the opportunity to donate. You can track progress, deadlines, and donors.
